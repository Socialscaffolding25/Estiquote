Section 02

About Estiquote

Estiquote is a UK home improvement cost intelligence platform. It provides two core services:

  • Cost Estimator: An interactive tool that generates indicative cost ranges for home improvement projects across eight trade categories, using regional UK pricing data updated monthly.

Estiquote is operated by SSH Ltd, a holding company registered in England and Wales. Estiquote is a trading brand of SSH Ltd.

Estiquote is not a construction company, contractor, or regulated financial or legal adviser. We do not carry out building work, manage projects, or guarantee the quality of any trade business listed on the platform.

Section 09

Cost Estimates — Important Disclaimer

⚠️ Estimates are indicative only
Estiquote cost estimates are indicative ranges based on regional UK pricing data and user inputs. They are not quotes, tenders, or binding contracts. Actual project costs may differ significantly depending on site-specific conditions, contractor availability, material prices at time of purchase, specification changes, and other factors outside our control.
9.1 What estimates are based on

Estiquote estimates are derived from the following data sources:

  • BCIS (Building Cost Information Service): Regional construction cost indices and trade labour rates.
  • BMF (Builders Merchant Federation): Merchant material pricing data.
  • ONS (Office for National Statistics): Construction output price index.
  • Internal calibration data based on submitted and verified project outcomes.

Material prices are updated monthly. Regional labour multipliers are reviewed quarterly.

9.2 Limitations

Estiquote estimates do not account for: site-specific access constraints, structural surveys, planning conditions, ground investigation findings, party wall obligations, unusual material specifications, contractor premiums, or any other project-specific factors. You should always obtain at least three written quotes from qualified contractors before committing to any project.

9.3 No professional advice

Nothing produced by Estiquote constitutes professional quantity surveying, structural engineering, architectural, financial, or legal advice. Estiquote does not accept liability for decisions made on the basis of its estimates. For significant capital expenditure, we strongly recommend engaging a qualified professional.

9.4 VAT on estimates

All Estiquote cost estimates are shown exclusive of VAT unless otherwise stated. Most UK building contractors charge VAT at 20% on labour and materials. Some projects may qualify for reduced VAT rates (for example, certain conversions and renovations). Always confirm VAT treatment with your contractor and seek professional advice where necessary.

Section 11

Map & Search

The Estiquote Builder Marketplace uses mapping data provided by OpenStreetMap contributors, delivered via Leaflet.js. Map data is licensed under the Open Database Licence.

Search results and map positioning are determined by a combination of subscription tier, distance from the user's search location, and profile completeness. Pro builder listings appear at the top of search results and receive priority map placement. Featured listings receive priority placement above Listed tier. Within each tier, results are ordered by distance from the homeowner's search point.

Estiquote does not guarantee any specific search ranking position. Rankings may change as new builders join or as algorithm improvements are made. We will give reasonable notice of any material changes to ranking logic.

Postcode-based search uses publicly available postcode data. Location accuracy is to postcode district level for Free users and postcode sector level for Pro and Trade users. Estiquote does not collect or store precise GPS location data without explicit consent.
