Questions

Everything builders ask

Can I really cancel any time?
Yes. Monthly billing, no annual commitment, no 14-day trap. Cancel from your dashboard and billing stops at the end of the current month. Your profile data is always yours to export.
Are the leads actually exclusive?
Yes. When a homeowner contacts you on Estiquote, that enquiry goes to you alone — not to 3 competitors simultaneously. You're not entering a race before you've even picked up the phone.
What happens to my reviews if I cancel?
They're yours. You can export all your reviews, project portfolio, and enquiry history at any time from your dashboard. Unlike Checkatrade, your reputation doesn't disappear when you stop paying.
How do homeowners find me?
After running a cost estimate, homeowners see a "Find builders in your area" section. They filter by trade and location — and your profile appears. These are high-intent users actively planning a project.
Will my Checkatrade reviews transfer?
We can't import them directly — they belong to Checkatrade. But we'll help you reach out to past clients to collect new verified reviews on Estiquote. Most builders have their profile reviews rebuilt within 4–6 weeks.
What areas does Estiquote cover?
We launched in SE England (Sussex, Surrey, Kent) and are expanding nationally. If you're outside our current coverage area, register now and you'll be live the moment we expand to your region — at founding member pricing.
