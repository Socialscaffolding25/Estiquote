Questions

Builder FAQ

When does this launch?
We're launching Estiquote with the homeowner cost estimator first. Builder listings and the local map follow in Phase 2. Register your interest and we'll email you the moment it opens.
Will I be charged anything today?
No. Registering your interest is free and doesn't require payment details. You'll only be asked to pay once listings go live and you choose to subscribe.
Is there a contract or minimum term?
No contract, no minimum term. Monthly rolling subscription. Cancel any time from your dashboard — you keep access until the end of the billing period.
What area will listings cover?
You'll appear in searches centred on your postcode, within the homeowner's chosen radius. You'll be able to set a coverage area of up to 50 miles from your base.
Will you check our insurance and qualifications?
Yes. We'll verify public liability insurance and relevant trade qualifications (Gas Safe, NICEIC, etc.) before awarding the Verified badge. This protects both you and homeowners.
What do I get for registering early?
Early registrants lock in founding member pricing and get first access when we open listings in your area.
