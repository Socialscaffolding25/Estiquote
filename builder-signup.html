Questions

Builder FAQ

How quickly will I appear on the map?
We verify all businesses within 24 hours of signup. Once verified, your pin goes live immediately and you start appearing in searches.
Is there a contract or minimum term?
No contract, no minimum term. Monthly rolling subscription. Cancel any time from your dashboard — you keep access until the end of the billing period.
What area do I appear in?
You appear in searches centred on your postcode, within the homeowner's chosen radius. You can set a coverage area of up to 50 miles from your base.
Do you check our insurance and qualifications?
Yes. We verify public liability insurance and relevant trade qualifications (Gas Safe, NICEIC, etc.) before awarding the Verified badge. This protects both you and homeowners.
Can I list multiple trades?
Yes — your profile can show all the trades you cover. You appear in searches for every trade you list, not just your primary one.
How do homeowners contact me?
Via a direct enquiry form that sends to your email and phone. Pro tier also gets a direct message inbox. We never sell or share your contact details with third parties.
